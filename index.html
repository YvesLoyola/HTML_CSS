How to cook Adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 6 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 pc onion, sliced
  • 1 kilogram chicken cut ups
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 pcs bay leaves
  • 1 tsp brown sugar packed
  • Option: 1 cup kale or spinach

Cook

    1. Heat oil in pan and saute garlic and onions. Then add chicken to the pan and sear on all sides, until you have a little browning in the chicken skin.
    2. Pour in vinegar, soy sauce and water. Add bay leaves, pepper and Knorr Chicken Cubes. Bring to a boil over high heat then reduce heat to simmer, but do not cover the pan. Continue to the simmer for 10 mins.
    3. Remove chicken pieces from the sauce and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
    4. Put back fried chicken pieces into sauce. Add sugar and let simmer again for another 10 mins or until sauce has thickened. Serve warm
